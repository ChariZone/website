Here's a question for you

Why do the largest charities, who only make up 10% of all charities, receive 90% of the UK's donations?

Even though 2022 plays host to more charities and non-profits than ever before, the organisations with the largest marketing budget still often end up with the lion’s share of the world’s donations.

That’s why we empower our clients to use their wealth for good, by collating insights and analytics on charities from across the globe into easily-digestible reports focused on three core areas: each charity’s true impact on the world, their governance, and their finances.

We're here to inspire philanthropists and wealth managers to give with confidence, and do good in the world.
